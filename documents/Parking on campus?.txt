emptysplashlog: Nah it’s not too bad until peak times in my experience. I generally park around 9:30 every morning no issues.

AlarmingPlate6504: Depends. If you are a morning person, no sweat. Most parking lots fill up between 9 and 10 a.m. and are usually the most packed during lunch, since a lot of the kids eat at their fraternity or sorority. It usually empties out by 2.

It has been a couple years, though, and with enrollment growing, I would think it may be a bit worse now. Best strategy if you are in a pinch is to sit by the walkway where people are returning from class and ask someone if you can take their spot if they are leaving. Saves you a couple minutes of making laps.
