Comments:

jopasm: Go to the Parking website, it will tell you what you need to know. Go ahead and buy the $5 visitor pass and you can park in any non-reserved spot on campus, and there is visitor-only parking set aside at the Circle (you still need a pass, though). The Circle is next to the Grove and is a good central parking area to start your exploration.

https://olemiss.edu/parking/visitors.html

There are a few metered spots but they're usually limited to short stays. If you stay at The Inn at Ole Miss (the on-campus hotel) you can park in their lot without additional fee.

https://www.theinnatolemiss.com/

What are you interested in? If you reach out to Campus Visits they might be able to arrange a tour and meeting with faculty or students in the program(s) you are looking at. https://olemiss.edu/admissions/visit-campus/
